What KrishiSetu does

One platform, from field to decision

Every response is captured, scored for vulnerability, and rolled up into live dashboards — giving officers, NGOs and policymakers a clear, current picture of farmer wellbeing.

📝

Structured Data Collection

A guided, multi-step survey captures demographics, farm profile, income, climate and finance signals — validated and stored as clean records.

📊

Live Analytics

Interactive charts break down vulnerability by district, education, crop and market access — updating the moment a new response lands.

🧾

Vulnerability Scoring

Each farmer gets an automatic 0–100 risk score across ten stress indicators, banded Low to Severe for fast triage.

👤

Farmer Directory

Search and filter every surveyed farmer, drill into an individual profile, and generate a shareable assessment report.

🏦

Scheme Awareness

Map farmers against government schemes like PMFBY, PM-KISAN and KCC to spot who is missing out on entitled support.

⚙

Frappe-Powered

Built as a full Frappe app — every survey, farmer, crop and scheme is a managed DocType, editable from the admin desk.
